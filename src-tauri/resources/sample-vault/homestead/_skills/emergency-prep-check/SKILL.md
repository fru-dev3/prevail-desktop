# Emergency prep check

Run twice a year — the calm before, not the scramble after.

1. **Safety devices.** Against data/maintenance-log.csv, confirm smoke and CO detectors tested, fire extinguisher in date, batteries fresh. Schedule any that lapsed this week.
2. **Supplies.** Verify the basics for a few days without power or water — water, food, flashlight, first aid, meds, cash. List what's missing or expired.
3. **Shutoffs and contacts.** Confirm everyone knows where the water, gas, and electrical shutoffs are, and that the emergency contact and utility numbers are written down, not just in one phone.
4. **Documents.** Check that insurance policies, IDs, and the home inventory are backed up and reachable if the house isn't. Note the gap.

Output: the device and supply gaps to fix, the shutoff/contact card, and the document backup status.
