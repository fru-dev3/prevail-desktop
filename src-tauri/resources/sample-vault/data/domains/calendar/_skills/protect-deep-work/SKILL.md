# Protect deep work

Run weekly, or whenever the days feel full but nothing real shipped.

1. **Where it went.** Against data/calendar.ics, total the deep-work hours that survived last week versus what was blocked. Meetings quietly eat the calendar; name the leak.
2. **Find the windows.** Identify next week's two or three blocks that can hold two unbroken hours each — protect the morning peak first, before others claim it.
3. **Set the rules.** For each block: no meetings, notifications off, one specific outcome attached. A block with no outcome gets traded away.
4. **Pre-commit the defense.** Decide in advance the polite line for declining the meeting that will try to land on a focus block. Saying it now is easier than in the moment.

Output: last week's focus-hour count, next week's protected blocks with outcomes, and the decline line.
