# Post-trip debrief

Run within a week of getting home, while the trip is still vivid.

1. **Best and worst.** The two or three moments that made the trip and the one that didn't. This is what tells you what kind of travel actually fits the family.
2. **Keep / skip.** What to repeat next time (a hotel, a pace, a packing choice) and what to drop. Write it where the next trip-blueprint will see it.
3. **Cost vs. plan.** Compare actual spend against the budget in data/trips/. Note where it ran over and why, so the next estimate is honest.
4. **File the memory.** A short note and the few photos worth keeping, linked to the trip — a life rich with places is one you can look back on.

Output: the keep/skip list, the budget variance, and a short trip note saved to data/trips/.
