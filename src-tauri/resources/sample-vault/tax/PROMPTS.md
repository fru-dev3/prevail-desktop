3. **Safe Harbor Payment Check:** "Calculate whether my estimated tax payments satisfy the IRS safe harbor rule. Read vault/tax/00_current/ for my prior year total tax liability. Apply safe harbor rules: (1) pay 100% of prior year tax if AGI was ≤$150,000, or (2) pay 110% of prior year tax if AGI was >$150,000. Compare total payments made YTD (withholding + estimated payments from vault/tax/00_current/) to the safe harbor threshold. If current payments fall short, compute the amount needed for the next estimated payment to restore safe harbor coverage. Output: prior year tax, safe harbor target, YTD payments, shortfall, and payment recommendation."

4. **Entity Filing Deadline Tracker:** "Read vault/tax/00_current/ for all business entities (LLC, S-corp, partnership). Confirm whether a Form 7004 extension was filed for any entity. List all entity-level payroll tax deposits (Form 941 due quarterly: Apr 30, Jul 31, Oct 31, Jan 31). Flag any entity with a missed or upcoming deadline within 60 days. Output a per-entity compliance calendar."

## Quarterly Estimates

5. **Quarterly Estimated Payment Calculator:** "Calculate my Q2 estimated tax payment due Jun 16. Calculate projected federal and state tax liability, subtract YTD withholding, and determine the estimated payment needed to maintain safe harbor (100% or 110% of prior year tax). Output: income projection, estimated liability, YTD payments, and recommended payment amount."

6. **Underpayment Penalty Risk Assessment:** "Assess my risk of an IRS underpayment penalty for the current tax year. Read vault/tax/00_current/ for prior year AGI and total tax. Apply: if prior year AGI ≤$150,000, safe harbor requires payments equaling 100% of prior year tax; if AGI >$150,000, safe harbor requires 110%. Pull total YTD withholding and estimated payments from vault/tax/00_current/. Compute: safe harbor target ÷ 4 for each quarterly installment, compare to what was actually paid each quarter. Flag any quarter where the installment payment was underpaid by >$1,000. Calculate the estimated IRS underpayment penalty using Form 2210 methodology. Output a per-quarter payment analysis and penalty estimate."

7. **SE Tax & QBI Deduction Estimate:** "Read vault/tax/00_current/ for self-employment income in the current year. Compute self-employment tax: 15.3% on the first $176,100 of net self-employment income (2025 Social Security wage base), plus 2.9% Medicare on income above that threshold, plus 0.9% Additional Medicare Tax if total income exceeds $200,000 (single) or $250,000 (MFJ). Apply the SE tax deduction: deduct 50% of SE tax from gross income. Calculate the QBI deduction: 20% of qualified business income from pass-through entities, subject to W-2 wage and qualified property limitations if taxable income exceeds $197,300 (single) or $394,600 (MFJ) in 2025. Output: net SE income, SE tax, QBI deduction estimate, and net tax impact."

8. **Annual Estimated Payment Summary:** "Produce a full-year estimated tax payment summary by reading vault/tax/00_current/ and vault/tax/00_current/. 13. **Home Office Deduction Calculator:** "Calculate my home office deduction using data from vault/tax/00_current/ and vault/tax/00_current/. Compute: business-use percentage = office sq ft ÷ total sq ft. Apply to total home expenses to determine the deductible amount. Compare simplified vs. regular method results. Flag the home office must be used regularly and exclusively for business. Output: both method calculations, recommended method, and required substantiation documentation."

14. **Charitable Contribution Summary:** "Compile all charitable contributions for the current tax year from vault/tax/00_current/.
