## Prompt

We're considering a 10-day family trip over the holidays to see relatives out of state. Can we afford it this year, and how should we plan it?

## Context

Alex and Jordan have family out of state. Household income $240k but the emergency fund is still ~4 months and the 529 is modestly funded. Maya is 6 (school calendar matters).

## Notes

A good answer says it is affordable if scoped to a defined budget that does not raid the emergency fund or stall the 529 contributions, anchors flights and lodging early, and aligns with Maya's school calendar (the winter break is the clean window).
