## Prompt

My next RSU vest (5,000 shares total over 4 years, ~$12,500 this year) hits 2026-08-15. TechFlow stock is up. Should I sell the vested shares or hold them?

## Context

Alex, 34, Senior SWE at TechFlow (Series B SaaS). Base $145k. Already heavily exposed to TechFlow through salary and ESPP. Emergency fund ~$10k, building toward a kid's college fund.

## Notes

A good answer defaults to sell-on-vest (a vest is income; holding is a fresh decision to over-concentrate in the employer), notes the existing salary+ESPP concentration, and routes proceeds to diversification or the current goal.
