## Prompt

We have only employer life insurance. With a kid and a mortgage, do we need more, and what kind?

## Context

Alex $145k + Jordan $95k. Mortgage $451,800. Maya is 6. Only group life through employers (~1x salary each).

## Notes

A good answer recommends level term life (not whole life) sized to replace income plus cover the mortgage and childrearing, for both earners, and notes group coverage is not portable.
