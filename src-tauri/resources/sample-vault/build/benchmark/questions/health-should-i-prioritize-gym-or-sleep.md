## Prompt

I'm averaging 6 hours of sleep and trying to add 30-minute morning workouts. Should I keep waking up early for the gym or sleep the extra 30 minutes?

## Context

Parent of a toddler, high-stress job, inconsistent sleep schedule. Current health goal is overall energy and long-term fitness. No medical conditions.

## Notes

A good answer should recommend prioritizing sleep (7-8 hours) over adding workouts when sleep is already below the recommended threshold, explain the compounding health cost of chronic sleep restriction, and suggest scheduling workouts at times that don't cut into sleep.
