## Prompt

My weeks are filling with meetings and the Staff packet work keeps slipping. How do I fix my calendar?

## Context

Senior SWE prepping a Staff promo packet (due Q4) on top of regular delivery and side consulting. Meetings are creeping up.

## Notes

A good answer says block two protected deep-work windows before anything else claims them, kill or shorten recurring meetings that produce no decision, and keep one buffer hour for overflow.
