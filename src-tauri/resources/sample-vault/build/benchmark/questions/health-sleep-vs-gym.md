## Prompt

I can reliably protect either an early gym session or an extra hour of sleep, not both. Which should win?

## Context

Alex averages under 7 hours during busy weeks; that is the main weak spot. Wants to build a sustainable routine, not an ambitious one that collapses.

## Notes

A good answer prioritizes sleep when it is below 7 hours: fixing sleep outranks adding morning training: and suggests later or shorter workouts that do not cost recovery.
