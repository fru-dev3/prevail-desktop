# Cross-domain loop sweep

Run when things feel scattered, or every two weeks regardless. This is the chief's job: nothing slips between domains.

1. **Gather.** Pull every domain's open-loops.md into one list — career, calendar, home, wealth, the lot. The point is the whole picture in one place.
2. **Find the orphans.** Flag loops that belong to no single domain or hand off between two (a home repair waiting on a wealth decision, a career move that needs a calendar block). Orphans are where things die.
3. **Age and stall.** Mark anything open more than 30 days. Decide for each: next action, hand it to the right domain, or close it as abandoned on purpose.
4. **One action each.** Every surviving loop leaves with a concrete next action and a home. A loop with no next action is just worry.

Output: the unified loop list, the orphans named, and a next action and owner for each live loop.
