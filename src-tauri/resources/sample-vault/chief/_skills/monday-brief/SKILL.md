# Monday brief

Run Monday morning across ALL domains, not just this one.

1. **Top three.** From every domain's goals and open tasks: the three things
   that matter most this week. Three, not seven.
2. **Open decisions.** List decisions waiting on me (from decision ledgers and
   threads). For each: what is blocking it and the cheapest way to unblock.
3. **Dropped on purpose.** Name what will NOT get attention this week, so it is
   a choice rather than a surprise.
4. **One risk.** The single thing most likely to blow up the week, and the
   pre-emptive move.

Output: the brief in four short sections, readable in two minutes.
