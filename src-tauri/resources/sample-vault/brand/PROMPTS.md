# AI Ready Life: Brand — Prompt Reference

## Monthly Reviews

1. **Monthly Brand Review:** "Run my monthly brand review. Read vault/brand/00_current/ for this month's follower growth rate across all platforms and compare to my 2%/month healthy-growth threshold. Pull post engagement rates from each channel and flag any platform where engagement dropped more than 15% month-over-month versus my baseline (LinkedIn 2–5%, Instagram 1–3%, Twitter/X below 1%). Check vault/brand/00_current/ for any brand mentions flagged as negative or urgent. Identify the top 3 performing posts by reach and explain what they have in common. Output as a brief with a green/yellow/red traffic-light status per channel and one specific corrective action for any red-status platform."

2. **Brand Analytics Summary:** "Build my brand analytics summary for the trailing 30 days. Read vault/brand/00_current/ and extract: total follower count and net new followers per platform, average engagement rate per platform benchmarked against healthy ranges (LinkedIn 2–5%, Instagram 1–3%, Twitter/X below 1%), and my Google Analytics organic sessions, bounce rate (flag if above 60%), and average session duration trend. Cross-reference with vault/brand/00_current/ for my stated growth goals and calculate percent-to-goal for each platform. Present as a ranked table showing best-to-worst performing channel with a single sentence explaining the key driver for each."

3. **Quarterly Brand Brief:** "Give me my quarterly brand brief. Read vault/brand/00_current/ for my defined brand pillars and audience positioning, then read vault/brand/00_current/ for the past 90 days of data. Calculate quarter-over-quarter follower growth rate across all platforms, identify which content pillars drove the top 20% of reach, and flag any pillar where less than 80% of published content stayed on-pillar. Check if my LinkedIn SSI score has moved and whether Google Search Console shows improving or declining impressions for my top 5 target keywords. Deliver a one-page brief with a 3-bullet executive summary, a ranked channel scorecard, and 2–3 strategic recommendations for next quarter."

## Analytics

4. **Engagement Rate Deep Dive:** "Analyze my engagement rate across all brand channels for the past 30 days. Read vault/brand/00_current/ and compute engagement rate per post (likes + comments + shares / impressions) for LinkedIn, Instagram, and Twitter/X. Flag any post where comments-to-likes ratio exceeded 5%, as that signals high engagement quality and thought leadership resonance. Identify the content format (video, text, carousel, image) with the highest median engagement rate per platform. Compare these rates to my prior 30-day baseline and surface the top 3 posts that outperformed their platform average by the largest margin. Output a ranked list of posts with format, topic, engagement rate, and a one-line note on why it likely performed well."

5. **Newsletter Analytics Report:** "What is my newsletter open rate and how does it compare to the 35% healthy benchmark? Read vault/brand/00_current/ for the last 4 newsletter issues and extract: open rate, click rate (benchmark: above 3%), and unsubscribe rate (flag if above 0.3%). Calculate my 4-week rolling average for each metric and identify the issue with the highest open rate — analyze its subject line, send day, and topic to surface what drove the spike. Compare subscriber list size this month to last month and calculate the net-growth percentage. Output a table of the last 4 issues with all three metrics highlighted green or red versus benchmark, plus 2 actionable recommendations."

6. **LinkedIn Follower Trend Analysis:** "How are my LinkedIn followers trending? Read vault/brand/00_current/ for the past 90 days of LinkedIn data and extract: weekly follower count, net new followers per week, profile views trend, and post impressions trend. Identify the week with the highest follower gain and cross-reference what content was published that week. Calculate whether my current follower growth rate (target: above 2%/month) is on track. Check vault/brand/00_current/ for my LinkedIn SSI score and flag if it has dropped below 60. Deliver a trend chart description in prose, net new followers this quarter, and a clear yes/no on whether I'm on track for my LinkedIn growth goal."

7. **Cross-Channel Analytics Synthesis:** "Build my analytics summary across all active brand channels for this month. Read vault/brand/00_current/ and consolidate: follower count and growth rate per platform, average engagement rate per platform (benchmark thresholds: LinkedIn 2–5%, Instagram 1–3%, Twitter/X below 1%), website organic sessions from Google Analytics, and newsletter open rate versus 35% benchmark. Rank all channels by a composite health score combining growth rate and engagement rate. Flag the single weakest channel and the single strongest channel. Output a consolidated dashboard-style summary with platform name, follower count, growth rate, engagement rate, health status, and a one-sentence priority action."

## Profile Audit

8. **Full Profile Consistency Audit:** "Audit my profile consistency across all channels. Read vault/brand/00_current/ for the canonical bio text, profile photo filename, handle, headline, and brand color codes. Then check each active platform (LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter/X, YouTube, website) for: exact bio match or acceptable variation, profile photo match, handle uniformity, and featured/pinned content alignment with current brand pillars. Flag any platform where the bio deviates materially from the canonical version, the profile photo differs, or the handle is inconsistent. Produce a checklist table with one row per platform, columns for bio/photo/handle/featured, and a green checkmark or red X per field, plus a prioritized fix list."

9. **LinkedIn Profile Completeness Check:** "Run a LinkedIn profile completeness audit for my @fru profile. Read vault/brand/00_current/ and check whether each of the following sections is filled and current: profile photo (matches canonical brand photo), headline (includes primary keyword and value proposition), about section (above 300 characters, ends with a call to action), featured section (at least 2 pinned items linking to my best content or product), experience section (all current roles have descriptions), and skills section (at least 5 endorsed skills). Cross-reference with my current LinkedIn SSI score from vault/brand/00_current/ and flag any section where improvement would most directly lift the SSI. Output a pass/fail checklist with one specific improvement recommendation per failed item."

10. **Brand Handle & Bio Sync Check:** "Are my bio, headshot, and handles consistent everywhere? Read vault/brand/00_current/ for the master handle list and canonical bio. Compare actual profiles on LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter/X, YouTube, and my website against those canonical values. Flag any mismatch in: handle spelling or capitalization, bio text that is more than 15 words different from the canonical version, or profile photos that appear visually different. Note any platform where my website/link-in-bio URL is outdated. Output a matrix table: platform, handle match (yes/no), bio match (yes/no), photo match (yes/no), link current (yes/no), and a sorted priority list of fixes ranked by audience size of the platform."

## Mentions & Monitoring

11. **Brand Mention Review:** "Are there any brand mentions flagged as significant this week? Read vault/brand/00_current/ for all mentions logged in the last 7 days. Classify each mention as positive, neutral-but-significant, or negative. For any negative mention, extract the source, the exact claim or sentiment, and the reach estimate. For any mention with more than 500 estimated impressions (positive or negative), flag it as high-priority. Check whether any mention involves a factual claim about me or my products that requires a public response. Output a sorted list: negatives first, then high-reach positives, then neutral, with source URL, sentiment, reach estimate, and a recommended action (respond/monitor/ignore) for each."

12. **Sentiment Trend Analysis:** "Analyze recent mentions of my brand keyword across all monitored sources. Read vault/brand/00_current/ for the trailing 30 days and calculate: total mention count, percentage positive vs. neutral vs. negative, and average mentions per week. Compare this month's mention volume to the prior month — flag any spike (more than 50% week-over-week increase) or drop (more than 30% week-over-week decrease). Identify the top 3 sources driving the most mentions and whether those sources are growing or declining. Flag any mention from a publication or account with more than 10,000 followers that I have not yet acknowledged. Output a sentiment dashboard with counts, trend direction, top sources, and a priority action list."

13. **Negative Mention Escalation Check:** "What are people saying about my brand that could harm my reputation? Read vault/brand/00_current/ and filter for any mention with sentiment classified as negative or mixed-negative. For each flagged mention, extract: the source name and estimated reach, the specific claim or criticism, the date it was posted, and whether I or my team has already responded. Assess whether the criticism is factual (requires correction), opinion-based (requires monitoring), or coordinated (requires escalation). Recommend a specific response action for each: public reply, DM outreach, ignore, or legal review. Output a table sorted by severity with a response recommendation and draft 1-sentence response for the top 2 items."

## Content Strategy

14. **Content Pillar Alignment Audit:** "Audit my content against my defined brand pillars and report on alignment percentage. Read vault/brand/00_current/ for my official content pillars and their target mix (e.g., 60% educational, 20% personal story, 20% promotional). Then read vault/brand/00_current/ for my last 30 days of published posts across all platforms. Classify each post by pillar and calculate the actual mix percentage. Flag any pillar that is more than 10 percentage points off-target from the defined mix. Identify the top-performing post in each pillar by engagement rate and use it to surface what resonates most within that pillar. Output an alignment table with target vs. actual percentage per pillar, plus 2 content mix adjustments to make next month."

15. **Next Content Topics Recommendation:** "What topics should I create next based on my analytics and brand strategy? Read vault/brand/00_current/ for my top 10 posts by engagement over the last 60 days and identify the common themes, formats, and audience signals in the highest performers. Read vault/brand/00_current/ for my defined content pillars and audience persona. Cross-reference with vault/brand/00_current/ to identify any questions or topics my audience is actively asking about. Generate a ranked list of 5 specific content ideas, each with: topic title, target platform, recommended format (video/post/newsletter/carousel), the data signal that supports it, and an estimated engagement outcome. Sort by expected reach impact."

## Growth

16. **Subscriber Goal Tracking:** "Am I on track for my subscriber and follower growth goals this quarter? Read vault/brand/00_current/ for my stated growth goals per platform (follower targets and timeline). Then read vault/brand/00_current/ for current follower counts and the week-over-week growth rate for each platform over the past 8 weeks. Calculate the required weekly growth rate to hit each goal by the target date and compare it to the current trajectory. Flag any platform where I am more than 20% behind the required pace. For each platform that is off-track, identify the most recent 2-week period with the best growth rate and analyze what drove it. Output a goal-tracking table with current count, target, pace-to-goal, and a specific tactic to accelerate each lagging platform."

17. **Fastest-Growing Channel Report:** "Which of my channels is growing fastest right now, and what can the others learn from it? Read vault/brand/00_current/ for the trailing 30 days and calculate net follower growth rate (%) and absolute net new followers for each platform. Rank platforms by growth rate. For the top-ranked platform, extract the 3 most common content characteristics among its top 5 posts (format, length, topic, posting time). Assess whether those characteristics are currently being applied to the slower-growing platforms. If not, identify the specific gap. Output a ranked growth table, a profile of the top-performing channel's content pattern, and 2–3 cross-platform tactics to replicate that pattern."

## SEO & Discoverability

18. **Keyword Ranking Status Check:** "What keywords am I ranking for and how are they trending? Read vault/brand/00_current/ for my Google Search Console data from the last 30 days. Extract my top 10 queries by impressions, noting the average position, click-through rate (CTR), and impression trend for each. Flag any keyword where I rank between position 5–15 (high opportunity to move to page 1) and where CTR is below 3% (indicating a title or meta description problem). Identify any query where impressions increased more than 30% month-over-month as an emerging topic signal. Output a ranked keyword table with query, position, impressions, CTR, and a recommended action (optimize title, build backlinks, create supporting content) per opportunity."

19. **Brand Keyword Ownership Audit:** "What brand keywords should I own and where am I losing visibility? Read vault/brand/00_current/ for my defined brand keyword list and target audience personas. Check vault/brand/00_current/ for current Google Search Console data to see whether my brand name, product names, and core topic keywords appear in the top 3 impressions-generating queries. Identify any brand keyword where a competitor or unrelated content is outranking me. For each gap, recommend a specific content or technical SEO action: create a dedicated landing page, add a FAQ schema, or publish a pillar post targeting that keyword. Output a gap table with keyword, current rank, who outranks me, and a recommended fix."

20. **Google Analytics Health Check:** "How is my website performing from an organic traffic and user experience perspective? Read vault/brand/00_current/ for the last 30 days of Google Analytics data and extract: total organic sessions, bounce rate (flag if above 60%), average session duration (flag if below 90 seconds), and top 5 landing pages by organic traffic. Calculate month-over-month change in organic sessions and flag any page where bounce rate exceeds 70% or session duration dropped more than 20 seconds. Cross-reference top landing pages with my content pillar mix to confirm traffic is coming from on-brand topics. Output a site health summary with green/yellow/red for each key metric and a prioritized list of 3 pages to improve first."

## Open Loops

21. **Brand Open Loop Triage:** "What brand action items are unresolved and which ones have been sitting the longest? Read vault/brand/open-loops.md and extract every open item with its creation date, domain sub-category (analytics, profile, mentions, content, growth, SEO), and any last-updated note. Calculate how many days each item has been open. Flag any item open more than 14 days as stale. Assess each item's potential impact: high (could affect audience trust or growth trajectory), medium (process improvement), or low (cosmetic or nice-to-have). Output a prioritized triage table sorted by impact then age, with a recommended next action for the top 5 items and a suggested close or defer decision for items older than 30 days."

22. **Brand Monthly Action Plan:** "Consolidate all brand signal data into a prioritized action plan for this month. Read vault/brand/00_current/ for channel health status, vault/brand/00_current/ for any unresolved reputation items, vault/brand/00_current/ for content pillar alignment gaps, and vault/brand/open-loops.md for any stale items older than 14 days. Identify the top 3 brand actions that would have the highest impact on audience growth or brand equity this month. For each action, specify: the task, the metric it will move, the time investment required, and the definition of done. Output as a numbered action plan with priority rank, expected outcome, and a suggested week within the month to execute."
