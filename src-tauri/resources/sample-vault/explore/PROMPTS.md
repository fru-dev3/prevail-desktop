## Document Status

4. **Travel Document Audit:** "Review all travel documents stored in vault/explore/00_current/ and flag expiration risks. For passport: most countries require 6 months of validity beyond the return date of any planned trip — check all upcoming trips in vault/explore/00_current/ against passport expiry and flag any conflict. For Global Entry and TSA PreCheck: both expire on a 5-year cycle — flag if expiry is within 6 months, as renewal applications should be submitted at least 6 months before expiry. For ESTA (visa waiver): valid 2 years or until passport expiry, whichever comes first — flag if expiring before any planned trip to a waiver country. Output a document status table with expiry date, days remaining, and red/yellow/green status."

5. **Passport Validity Check:** "Using the passport expiry date from vault/explore/00_current/ and the planned trips in vault/explore/00_current/, check whether my passport will be valid for every upcoming trip. Apply the 6-months-beyond-return-date rule used by the majority of international destinations. For trips where the passport is valid but has fewer than 9 months of validity remaining, flag as a watch item — passport renewal typically takes 6–8 weeks standard or 2–3 weeks expedited. If passport renewal is needed, estimate the latest date to apply to avoid disrupting planned trips. Output a trip-by-trip passport validity check and a renewal timeline recommendation if needed."

6. **Global Entry and PreCheck Status:** "Check the Global Entry and TSA PreCheck status from vault/explore/00_current/. Both programs operate on a 5-year renewal cycle. Renewal applications should be submitted at least 6 months before expiry to avoid a lapse in benefits, as processing can take several months. If either program expires before any planned trip in vault/explore/00_current/, flag as urgent. Also check: is Global Entry linked to my current passport? If passport was renewed without updating Global Entry, benefits at automated kiosks may not work. Output expiry dates, days remaining, renewal deadline recommendation, and any linking issues."

7. **ESTA and Visa Requirements:** "For every upcoming international trip in vault/explore/00_current/, check whether a visa or ESTA is required for entry. For ESTA-eligible countries (Visa Waiver Program): verify ESTA is valid (2-year validity, tied to current passport) and not expiring before the trip return date. For non-VWP countries: identify whether a tourist visa is required, the typical processing time, and the application deadline relative to travel dates. Flag any trip where a required visa or ESTA has not been confirmed. Output a per-trip entry requirements table with status (confirmed/pending/required) and action items."

## Trip Planning

8. **Trip Readiness Check:** "Using the trip record in vault/explore/00_current/, run a full pre-departure readiness check for my next upcoming trip. Assess readiness across 8 categories: (1) flights booked; (2) accommodations confirmed; (3) passport valid with 6+ months beyond return date; (4) visa or ESTA confirmed; (5) travel insurance active with trip cancellation and medical coverage; (6) credit card with no foreign transaction fees identified for this trip; (7) international data plan or SIM arranged; (8) vaccination requirements checked via CDC travel health notices. For each category show status (complete/in-progress/missing) and the specific action to take. Output a departure checklist sorted by urgency."

9. **Trip Budget Builder:** "Build a detailed trip budget for the trip in vault/explore/00_current/. Break costs into categories: flights, accommodations, ground transportation (airport transfers, car rental, rideshare), food (per-day estimate by destination: budget $50/day, mid-range $100/day, high-end $200+/day), activities and entrance fees, travel insurance (typically 4–8% of total prepaid trip costs), and miscellaneous (tips, souvenirs, pharmacy). Sum to a total estimated cost. Flag if the total exceeds $3,000 — trips at this level warrant travel insurance. Compare total estimated cost to any trip budget I have stored. Output a categorized budget table with estimated and actual spend columns."

10. **Trip Summary:** "Build a comprehensive trip summary for a specified trip in vault/explore/00_current/. Include: destination overview, travel dates and total trip duration, flights (airline, departure/arrival times, confirmation numbers), accommodations (hotel/Airbnb name, address, check-in/check-out dates, confirmation numbers), local transportation plan, restaurant reservations, major activities and tickets booked, total estimated cost, and outstanding items still to confirm. Format as a clean one-page trip sheet I can reference during travel."

11. **Pre-Departure Checklist:** "Generate a personalized pre-departure checklist for the next upcoming trip in vault/explore/00_current/, organized by time horizon. 30 days out: confirm all bookings, check passport validity, apply for visa/ESTA if needed, purchase travel insurance. 2 weeks out: contact credit card companies about international travel, arrange international data plan, research local currency and ATM access strategy, check CDC health advisories. 3 days out: download offline maps and boarding passes, confirm accommodation check-in details, pack medications. Day of: charge all devices, carry copies of passport and confirmation numbers, arrive at airport 2 hours domestic / 3 hours international. Flag any items not yet completed based on current date relative to departure."

Flag any program with points expiring within 90 days — these require either a redemption or a qualifying transaction to extend expiry. Compute total estimated cash value of all loyalty balances combined. Output a ranked table sorted by expiring-soonest."

15. **Best Credit Card for This Trip:** "For the next upcoming trip in vault/explore/00_current/, identify which credit card to use as the primary card based on travel benefits.
