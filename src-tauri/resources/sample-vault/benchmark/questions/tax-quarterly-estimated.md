## Prompt

My consulting income (~$22k, 1099, no withholding) is growing. Do I need to make quarterly estimated tax payments, or can I just settle in April?

## Context

W2 income has withholding; consulting is 1099 with none. Filing married jointly, Texas (no state income tax). CPA Maria Gonzalez.

## Notes

A good answer says yes: set aside and pay quarterly to avoid an underpayment penalty, explains the safe-harbor floor (prior-year based), and notes self-employment tax applies on the consulting net.
