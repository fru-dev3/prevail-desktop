## Prompt

Our single Carrier HVAC unit was installed in 2016 and is struggling. CoolBreeze quoted $8,900 for a full replacement. A repair would run around $1,200 but the tech says the compressor is near end of life. Should we repair or replace?

## Context

Austin summer ahead, a mid-July trip out of town, $32,000 emergency fund, homeowners renewal in September.

## Notes

The decision we actually made: replace. A 10-year-old unit failing in an Austin summer with the family away is the worst case, and a second repair on a dying compressor is money burned.
