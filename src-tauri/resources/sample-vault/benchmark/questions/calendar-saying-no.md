## Prompt

I keep getting pulled into things that aren't my priorities. How do I push back without looking uncommitted?

## Context

Alex prepping a Staff packet plus delivery plus consulting; meetings and asks are crowding out the priority work.

## Notes

A good answer gives a framework for declining gracefully (tie the no to current priorities, offer an alternative or a later slot, delegate where possible) and protecting the Staff-packet time as non-negotiable.
