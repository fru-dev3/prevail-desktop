## Prompt

Maya is 3 years old. Should I open a 529 for her education savings or put that money in a Roth IRA instead?

## Context

No 529 opened yet. Roth IRA is already being funded for the adults. Childcare costs are high right now. Looking to start with small monthly contributions ($50-100/month).

## Notes

A good answer should recommend opening a 529 (state tax deduction potential, dedicated education savings, SECURE 2.0 Roth rollover option if unused), note that Roth IRA for the child requires earned income, and clarify the Roth IRA comparison is about the adults' Roth, not Maya's.
