## Prompt

Spare $300 a month: keep it going to our kid's 529, or redirect it to prepay our 6.25% mortgage? The mortgage rate makes prepayment feel like a guaranteed return.

## Context

Kid is 6, so a 12-year horizon for college money. 529 balance $18,500. Mortgage balance $451,800, 30-year fixed from 2023.

## Notes

Our call: keep the 529. The tax-advantaged growth over 12 years plus financial-aid treatment beats a 6.25% guaranteed return on money we may want flexibility on, and we can revisit if rates let us refinance.
