## Prompt

We own a home in Austin and have two cars. Should we add a $1M umbrella liability policy?

## Context

Current auto and homeowners policies are in place but umbrella coverage is missing. Household income $168,000, growing net worth. A $1M umbrella policy typically costs $150-300/year.

## Notes

A good answer should recommend adding the umbrella policy given the asset level and low cost, explain what umbrella covers (liability beyond auto/home limits), and note the typical premium is minimal relative to the protection.
