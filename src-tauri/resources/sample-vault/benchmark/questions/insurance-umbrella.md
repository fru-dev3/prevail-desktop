## Prompt

We have about $423,000 net worth, a rental condo with a tenant, a 6-year-old, and a dog. We carry home, auto, landlord, and term life, but no umbrella policy. Is a $1M umbrella worth it this year, or should the money go to the 529 instead?

## Context

Umbrella quotes for our profile run $200 to $380 a year. The 529 already gets $300 a month automatically.

## Notes

The decision we stand behind: buy the umbrella. A tenant plus a dog plus a kid is exactly the liability profile umbrellas exist for, and the premium is a rounding error next to the tail risk.
