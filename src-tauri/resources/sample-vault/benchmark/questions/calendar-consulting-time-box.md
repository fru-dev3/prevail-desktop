## Prompt

My side consulting bleeds into family evenings. How do I contain it without dropping clients?

## Context

Alex Rivera Consulting LLC, three clients. Family with a 6-year-old. Consulting is the long-term plan but cannot cost the present.

## Notes

A good answer recommends fixed, communicated consulting windows (specific evenings or weekend blocks), protecting family time as a hard boundary, and setting client expectations to those windows.
