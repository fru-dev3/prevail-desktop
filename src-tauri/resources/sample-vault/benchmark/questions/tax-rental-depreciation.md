## Prompt

I report my Phoenix rental on Schedule E. Am I handling depreciation right, and what should I watch for?

## Context

Phoenix condo bought 2021 for $235k, now ~$268k, rented at $1,350/mo, net +$170/mo, 8% PM fee, managed by Sun Valley PM. Reported on Schedule E.

## Notes

A good answer confirms residential rental is depreciated over 27.5 years on the building (not land) value, that it offsets rental income, and flags depreciation recapture on eventual sale.
