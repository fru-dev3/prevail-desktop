## Prompt

My 2016 HVAC is reaching end-of-life and it's about to be peak Texas summer. The replacement quote is $6,850. My emergency fund is $10,240. Should I replace it now or wait?

## Context

Household in Austin, TX. Two incomes totaling $168,000. Emergency fund covers about 3 months of expenses. Replacing before failure avoids emergency pricing and downtime during peak heat.

## Notes

A good answer should recommend replacing now, cite the risk of emergency failure costs and summer timing, and note that the emergency fund can absorb it without touching investments.
