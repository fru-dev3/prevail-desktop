## Prompt

My LDL came back at 142, up from 135 last year and 128 the year before. My father has high cholesterol. Do I push for medication now or try lifestyle changes first?

## Context

34 years old, BP 118/76, training for a half-marathon, no other risk factors. Doctor is open to either path.

## Notes

What we actually did: three months of diet changes (less saturated fat, more soluble fiber) plus the marathon training, then an August recheck. Medication is the next step if the trend does not bend.
