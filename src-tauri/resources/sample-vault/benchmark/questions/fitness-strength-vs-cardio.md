## Prompt

I mostly run. Should I add strength training, and how do I fit it in without overdoing it?

## Context

Runs 3x/week, no current strength work. Limited time, kid and demanding job. Goal is durable health, not maximal performance.

## Notes

A good answer recommends adding two short full-body strength sessions a week for injury resilience and metabolic health, kept light enough not to wreck running recovery, and scheduled around the long run.
