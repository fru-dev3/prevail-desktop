## Prompt

Open enrollment is coming. High-deductible plan with an HSA, or the richer PPO?

## Context

Two-income household, one young kid (Maya), generally healthy, building tax-advantaged savings. Employer offers both.

## Notes

A good answer ties the choice to expected medical usage: for a healthy family the HDHP+HSA often wins on total cost plus the triple-tax-advantaged HSA, but the PPO can be right in a known high-utilization year.
