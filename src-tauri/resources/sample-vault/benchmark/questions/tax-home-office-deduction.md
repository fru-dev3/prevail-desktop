## Prompt

I do my consulting work from a home office and also take remote work days there. Can I deduct the home office, and how?

## Context

Alex has a dedicated home office at the Austin house used for Alex Rivera Consulting LLC (1099, Schedule C) and remote W2 days. CPA is Maria Gonzalez.

## Notes

A good answer confirms the deduction is available for the consulting (Schedule C) use if the space is used regularly and exclusively for business, explains the simplified vs actual-expense methods, and flags that W2 remote use alone does not qualify.
