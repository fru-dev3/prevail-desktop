## Prompt

My knee is niggling after the last few runs. Push through the plan or back off?

## Context

Alex building toward a half marathon, history of ambitious plans collapsing. Knee discomfort over recent runs, no acute injury.

## Notes

A good answer says back off now: reduce volume/intensity and cross-train while it settles, because pushing a niggle into an injury costs far more training than a few easy days.
