## Prompt

Rates are floating around. Should I refinance the primary mortgage, and when would it make sense?

## Context

Primary home $451,800 at 6.25% fixed, bought May 2023. PITI $3,180/mo, ~$33k equity.

## Notes

A good answer says only refinance when the new rate beats 6.25% by enough to clear closing costs within a sensible breakeven (roughly 0.75–1%+ lower), and to watch rates rather than act now.
