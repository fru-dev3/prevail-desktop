# AI Ready Life: Learning — Prompt Reference

## Monthly Reviews

1. **Learning Monthly Review:** "Pull every active course and book from vault/learning/00_current/ and calculate three metrics for each: days since last logged session, estimated weeks to completion based on current pace, and on-track vs. behind-target status. Flag any item stale for more than 10 days as at-risk and any item behind schedule by more than 3 weeks as critical. Cross-reference vault/learning/00_current/config.md to confirm my active stack matches my stated target skills. Summarize findings in a dashboard table and close with a single prioritized action to unblock the most critical item."

2. **Learning Quarter Snapshot:** "Review vault/learning/00_current/ and vault/learning/01_prior/ to produce my Q-to-date learning ledger. For each domain — technical skills, certifications, books, soft skills — report: hours invested, completions, and hours remaining. Compare weekly hours to my 5-hour deliberate-learning benchmark from vault/learning/00_current/config.md. Calculate my completion rate (completions ÷ items started) and flag if it is below the 15% industry average for online courses. Output a table plus a one-sentence verdict on whether I am on pace for my annual learning goal."

3. **Learning Brief:** "Generate a concise weekly learning brief using vault/learning/00_current/ as the source of truth. Structure it in three sections: (1) What I am currently learning — one line per active item showing platform, percent complete, and last session date. (2) This week's priority — the single course or book I should touch first based on urgency and scheduled exam or target date. (3) Open loops — any learning-related action items that are unresolved. Keep the entire brief under 300 words and end with a clear 'Top Action This Week' callout."

## Course Progress

4. **Active Course Status Check:** "Read vault/learning/00_current/ and produce a structured status card for each in-progress course. For every course include: platform, start date, percent complete, last session date, target completion date, weekly session hours, and a RAG (Red/Amber/Green) status. Mark Red if last session is more than 10 days ago or completion is more than 3 weeks behind target. Mark Amber if last session is 5–10 days ago or 1–3 weeks behind. Mark Green if on track. Sort the output Red first, then Amber, then Green."

5. **Course Prioritization Engine:** "Using vault/learning/00_current/ and vault/learning/00_current/config.md, rank all active courses by a composite priority score: weight exam or completion deadline at 40%, alignment to high-priority skill gaps at 35%, and days since last session at 25%. Output a ranked list with the score breakdown per course. Recommend the one course I should focus on exclusively this week and estimate the minimum weekly hours needed to hit its nearest milestone."

6. **Stale Course Intervention:** "Scan vault/learning/00_current/ for any course where the last logged session is more than 30 days ago — these are at-risk by industry standards where abandonment spikes after 30 days of inactivity. For each stale course produce: (1) days since last session, (2) estimated hours to completion, (3) a binary recommendation — restart or archive. For restart candidates, suggest a specific minimal re-entry point (e.g., 'resume at Module 4, Quiz 2'). For archive candidates, move the item to vault/learning/00_current/ and note the reason."

7. **Weekly Session Planner:** "Using vault/learning/00_current/ and my 5-hour weekly learning target from vault/learning/00_current/config.md, draft a concrete time-block plan for the next 7 days. Allocate hours across active courses proportionally to deadline urgency. For each session block specify: course name, topic to cover, duration, and target output (e.g., 'complete Module 3 and pass practice quiz'). Output the plan as a Monday–Sunday schedule table and calculate the total planned hours to verify the 5-hour minimum is met."

## Reading

8. **Reading Pipeline Audit:** "Review vault/learning/00_current/ for all books currently in progress and vault/learning/01_prior/ for books finished in the last 90 days. For in-progress books report: title, pages read, percent complete, and days since last reading session. For completed books report: whether a takeaway summary was logged within 48 hours of completion — if not, flag it as a retention gap, since research shows 80%+ recall requires summarizing within 48 hours. Close with a recommendation: which book to finish first and which completed book needs a retroactive summary."

9. **Next Book Recommendation:** "Read vault/learning/00_current/ for all books on my reading backlog and vault/learning/00_current/config.md for my current skill-development priorities. Filter the wishlist to books that address at least one high-priority skill gap. From that filtered set, recommend the top three books ranked by: skill gap criticality at 50%, estimated read time fit with my current weekly reading pace at 30%, and author credibility or review score at 20%. Output the ranked list with a one-sentence justification for each recommendation."

10. **Book Retention Review:** "For every book completed in the last 6 months in vault/learning/01_prior/, assess retention quality. Check whether a summary, key takeaways, or action items were logged within 48 hours of the finish date. For books with no logged summary, generate a spaced-repetition recall prompt: list 5 questions the reader should be able to answer if they retained the core content. Flag any book where no summary exists and completion was more than 48 hours ago — these are at-risk for knowledge decay. Output a retention scorecard by book."

11. **Spaced Repetition Schedule:** "Using vault/learning/01_prior/, identify all material completed in the last 30 days. Apply the spaced repetition intervals — review at 1 day, 7 days, and 30 days post-completion. For each completed item, calculate which review interval is due or overdue as of today. Output a review schedule table sorted by urgency showing: course or book title, completion date, next review due date, and review type (day-1 scan, day-7 quiz, day-30 deep recall). Flag any overdue reviews in red."

## Skills Gap

12. **Skill Gap Analysis vs. Target Role:** "Read vault/learning/00_current/config.md for my target role and current skill inventory. Identify skills that appear in more than 60% of job descriptions for that role — treat these as Tier-1 required skills. Cross-reference against my current skill list and active learning stack in vault/learning/00_current/. Flag any Tier-1 skill with no active learning resource assigned as a critical gap. Output a gap matrix: Skill | Frequency in JDs | Current Level | Active Resource | Gap Severity. Sort by Gap Severity descending."

13. **Learning Roadmap Builder:** "Using the gap matrix from vault/learning/00_current/config.md and the wishlist in vault/learning/00_current/, construct a 12-month learning roadmap to close all Tier-1 skill gaps. Sequence learning items so that foundational skills are acquired before advanced ones. For each item include: resource name, format (course/book/certification), estimated hours, target completion month, and expected skill level gained. Flag any gap with no identified resource in vault/learning/00_current/ as an open loop requiring a resource search."

14. **Competitive Skill Positioning:** "Review vault/learning/01_prior/ for all certifications, courses, and books finished in the last 24 months. Map each to its market signal value: AWS Solutions Architect carries a documented +15% salary premium, PMP adds approximately +10%, CFA adds a premium in finance roles. For any completed certification that is more than 3 years old, flag it as potentially outdated for hiring signal purposes. Produce a credentials summary table showing: credential, completion date, estimated market premium, and recency status."

15. **Employer Benefit Utilization Check:** "Read vault/learning/00_current/config.md for my employer's tuition reimbursement and learning benefit details. Calculate how much of the annual benefit I have consumed versus the total available, and how many months remain in the benefit year. Flag any high-value courses in vault/learning/00_current/ that could be covered by remaining benefit budget. If the remaining budget is more than $200 and more than 30 days remain in the benefit period, output a prioritized list of 3 courses to submit for reimbursement before the deadline."

## Certifications

16. **Certification Pipeline Status:** "Scan vault/learning/00_current/ for all active certification tracks. For each certification output: exam name, governing body, exam date if scheduled, percent of prep material completed, recommended study hours remaining based on exam proximity, and a preparation intensity rating (Low/Moderate/High/Critical). Apply known prep timelines as benchmarks: AWS certifications require approximately 3 months at 5 hours/week, PMP requires approximately 6 months, CPA requires approximately 18 months across 4 sections. Flag any certification where actual pace is less than 60% of required pace."

17. **Certification ROI Ranking:** "Using vault/learning/00_current/ for pending certifications and vault/learning/00_current/config.md for my target role, rank all candidate certifications by return on investment. Score each on: salary premium percentage (40%), alignment to target role Tier-1 skills (35%), and time to complete relative to my current weekly study hours (25%). Output a ranked table showing certification name, estimated salary premium, alignment score, months to complete at current pace, and composite ROI score. Recommend the single best certification to pursue next and state the reason."

18. **Exam Readiness Assessment:** "For the active certification in vault/learning/00_current/ closest to its exam date, run a readiness assessment. Calculate: percent of curriculum completed, number of practice exams taken and average score, days remaining until exam, and required daily study hours to cover remaining material. If the practice exam average is below 75%, flag the exam as not-ready and recommend postponement. If above 75% but below 85%, flag as borderline and list the 3 topic domains with the lowest practice scores to prioritize. Output a one-page readiness brief."

## Learning Goals

19. **Weekly Learning Hour Audit:** "Read vault/learning/00_current/ for session logs from the past 7 days. Total the hours logged and compare against the 5-hour weekly deliberate learning benchmark. Calculate my 4-week rolling average to distinguish a one-off dip from a trend. If the weekly total is below 3 hours, flag as a significant shortfall. If the rolling 4-week average is below 4 hours, flag as a systemic capacity problem and recommend a schedule adjustment. Output: this week's hours, 4-week rolling average, benchmark comparison, and a one-line diagnosis."

20. **Quarterly Learning Goal Review:** "Pull my Q-to-date learning goals from vault/learning/00_current/config.md. For each stated goal, assess progress on a 0.0–1.0 OKR scale: 0.0 = not started, 0.7 = mostly complete, 1.0 = fully achieved. Flag any goal below 0.4 with more than 50% of the quarter elapsed as at-risk. For at-risk goals, output a recovery plan stating the minimum weekly hours and specific next step needed to finish by quarter-end. Summarize the quarter's overall learning health score as the average of all KR scores."

21. **Annual Learning Ledger:** "Compile a year-to-date learning summary using vault/learning/01_prior/ and vault/learning/00_current/. Count: total courses completed, total books read, certifications earned, and estimated total learning hours invested. Compare each metric to my annual targets in vault/learning/00_current/config.md. Calculate achievement rate for each target as actual ÷ target × 100%. Flag any annual target below 50% achievement with more than 6 months of the year elapsed. Output a ledger table and a summary paragraph projecting end-of-year achievement at current pace."

## Log & Complete

22. **Course Completion Log:** "Log a course completion to vault/learning/01_prior/. Capture the following fields: course name, platform, completion date, total hours invested, top 3 key takeaways, skills unlocked, and whether a completion certificate was earned and stored. After logging, cross-reference vault/learning/00_current/config.md to determine whether this completion closes any Tier-1 skill gap. If yes, mark that gap as closed and update the skill inventory. If no, note which open gaps remain and recommend the next resource to close the highest-priority one."

23. **Book Completion Log with Retention Capture:** "Log a book completion to vault/learning/01_prior/ using the following structure: title, author, finish date, genre or domain, key takeaways (minimum 3 specific insights, not vague summaries), one action or behavior change to apply within 30 days, and a 1–10 recommend-to-a-peer score. Flag this entry for a spaced repetition review at 7 days and 30 days post-completion. If the finish date was more than 48 hours ago, add a retention-risk note and ask the user to complete a 5-question recall check before logging."

24. **Wishlist Curation:** "Read vault/learning/00_current/ and audit the current backlog. Remove or archive any item added more than 12 months ago with no progress or engagement update — stale wishlist items signal intent drift. For each remaining item, assign a priority tier: Tier-1 if it addresses a current skill gap, Tier-2 if it is aspirational but not urgent, Tier-3 if it is purely exploratory. Output a cleaned wishlist sorted by priority tier, with items older than 6 months flagged for confirmation that they are still relevant to current goals."

## Open Loops

25. **Learning Open Loops Triage:** "Scan vault/learning/ across all subdirectories for any flagged action items, incomplete logging entries, overdue reviews, or unresolved decisions. Categorize each open loop by type: (1) Stale course — needs restart or archive decision, (2) Missing summary — completed item with no takeaways logged, (3) Overdue spaced repetition review, (4) Benefit expiry — employer learning budget at risk, (5) Exam scheduling — certification prep complete but no exam date booked. Output a prioritized triage list sorted by urgency and estimated resolution time in minutes."

26. **Learning Debt Clearance Plan:** "Define learning debt as any item in vault/learning/00_current/ that has been started but untouched for more than 21 days, or any item in vault/learning/01_prior/ with no summary or takeaways logged. Calculate total learning debt across both categories. For each debt item produce a recommended resolution: (a) complete and summarize within 7 days, (b) schedule a specific restart session, or (c) archive with a brief note on why. Output a debt clearance schedule with estimated time per item and a total hours-to-clear-debt figure."

27. **Learning System Health Check:** "Perform a full audit of the learning vault structure across vault/learning/00_current/, vault/learning/01_prior/, and vault/learning/00_current/. Check: (1) All active items have a last-session date logged within 30 days. (2) All completed items have a takeaway summary. (3) The wishlist has been reviewed and pruned in the last 90 days. (4) The skill inventory in vault/learning/00_current/config.md was updated after the last completion. (5) My 5-hour weekly average is documented for the past 4 weeks. Score each check Pass/Fail and output a system health scorecard with specific remediation steps for each failure."
