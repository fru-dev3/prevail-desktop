# Comp-review prep

Use before any compensation or performance conversation.

1. **Anchor on total comp.** Pull current base, bonus, and unvested equity into
   one number. Benchmark it against market for the role and level.
2. **Lead with the right lever.** When the original equity grant is partway
   vested and the company is doing well, an RSU refresh usually beats a base
   bump: recurring, performance-linked, tax-advantaged upside. Keep base as a
   fallback ask.
3. **Build the evidence file.** List shipped impact, scope growth, and any
   market data. Quantify wherever possible.
4. **Set the ask and the floor.** Decide the target, the acceptable floor, and
   the walk-away. Write the one-sentence opening ask.
5. **Time it.** Tie the conversation to a review cycle or a concrete win, not a
   random week.

Output the recommended lever, the target/floor numbers, and the opening line.
