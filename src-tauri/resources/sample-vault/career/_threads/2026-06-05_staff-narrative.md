## You

Help me frame my search-relevance project as a Staff-level story for my August review.

## codex · gpt-5

Staff stories are about scope and leverage, not just shipping. Frame it in three beats: the ambiguous problem you defined (not just executed), the cross-team alignment you drove, and the durable impact (a metric plus a pattern others now reuse). What was the headline metric, and who outside your team changed how they work because of it?
