# 1Password — Quickstart

Credentials live here, not in plaintext files.

## Common openers

- *"Fetch the Mercury bank login"* — uses `op` CLI to retrieve from Business vault
- *"Audit items I haven't touched in over a year"* — surfaces stale entries
- *"Generate and save a new credential for <service>"* — creates the item
  with strong password + stores recovery codes
- *"Verify all business critical items have 2FA backup codes attached"*
