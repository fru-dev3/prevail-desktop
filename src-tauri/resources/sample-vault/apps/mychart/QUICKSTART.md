# MyChart — Quickstart

The medical portal. Use it when you need clinical truth, not your memory.

## Common openers

- *"Pull the latest lab results"* — fetches most recent panel + flags
- *"What appointments do I have in the next 60 days?"*
- *"When is the lisinopril refill due?"*
- *"Read the notes from my last visit with Dr. Patel"*
- *"Schedule the cardiology consult — find the earliest slot after Apr 15"*
