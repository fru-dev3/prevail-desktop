# Race readiness check

Run four weeks before any race (see the half-marathon thread).

1. **Evidence.** Longest recent run, average weekly volume, and the pace of the
   last quality session. Readiness is what the log says, not how today felt.
2. **Projection.** Realistic race pace from current fitness, with a stretch and
   a safe target. Commit to starting at the safe one.
3. **Taper.** Plan the final three weeks: volume down roughly 30/50/70 percent,
   intensity kept, sleep protected.
4. **Logistics.** Gear tested on a long run, fueling rehearsed, nothing new on
   race day.

Output: readiness verdict, the two paces, the taper calendar, the checklist.
