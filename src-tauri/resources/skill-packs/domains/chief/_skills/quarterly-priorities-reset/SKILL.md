# Quarterly priorities reset

Run at the quarter turn, across ALL domains. The zoom-out the weekly cadence can't give you.

1. **Score the soul.** Read each domain's soul.md north star and judge honestly: closer or drifting this quarter? One line of evidence per domain, not a feeling.
2. **What the quarter taught.** Name the pattern across domains, where time and attention actually went versus where they were meant to go. The gap is the lesson.
3. **Three bets.** Choose the three priorities for next quarter that move the north stars most. Across all of life, three, anything more is a wish list.
4. **Protect and prune.** For each bet, name what gets the calendar and what gets dropped to make room. A new priority with no sacrifice doesn't happen.

Output: per-domain north-star score, the quarter's lesson, and the three bets with their trade-offs.
