# Promotion case builder

Run a cycle ahead of the promotion conversation, not the week of.

1. **Rubric first.** Write out the next level's expectations — scope, autonomy, impact, influence. Without the bar, evidence is just a list.
2. **Map the evidence.** Pull wins from data/brag-doc.md and _state.md and slot each under the rubric line it proves. The case is the mapping, not the pile.
3. **Name the gaps.** Where a rubric line has thin or no evidence, say so plainly. A gap surfaced now is a quarter to fix it; a gap surfaced in the room is a denial.
4. **Line up sponsors.** Identify who will advocate in the room and what one-line summary you need each to be able to repeat about your work.
5. **The narrative.** Write the three-sentence story: already operating at the level, here is the proof, here is the ask.

Output: the rubric-to-evidence map, the named gaps with fixes, and the three-sentence case.
